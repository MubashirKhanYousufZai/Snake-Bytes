# About the Author

**Mubashir Khan Yousufzai** is a passionate **Web Developer and Tech Enthusiast** from Pakistan.  

He completed his school education at **Metropolitan Academy II**, specializing in **Computer Science**, and has been actively advancing his technical skills through hands-on projects and skill enhancement programs.

In **January 2024**, Mubashir enrolled in a prestigious **skill enhancement program launched by Governor Sindh, Kamran Khan Tessori**, focusing on **web development**. Through this program, he gained over **7 months of experience** in creating **modern web applications** and mastering **frontend and backend technologies**.

Mubashir has built several **full-stack projects**, including:

- **The Pawfect Store** – A user-friendly web app for pet lovers.  
- **Quick Crave** – A Q-commerce platform for a cafe.  
- **Pizza Run** – A restaurant ordering web app.  
- **Urdu Rap And Reality** – A blog website.  
- **Resume Builder** – Personalized resume creation web app.  

He specializes in:

- **Next.js**, **React**, **Tailwind CSS** for frontend development  
- **Node.js**, **Python**, **FastAPI**, and **Streamlit** for backend and data-driven applications  
- **UI/UX Design** and **Prompt Engineering**  

Mubashir is continuously exploring **new technologies, building innovative projects**, and sharing knowledge through tutorials and practical guides. His work reflects a commitment to **clean code, professional design, and real-world application development**.
